To play this game you must move between locations and interact with objects by typing one or two word commands or clicking on them.

Some commands are motion commands. These will move you from room to room. Motion commands include:

UP or 'W', DOWN or 'S', RIGHT or 'D', LEFT or 'A', aside '' are used to represent button clicks

Not all motions are possible in every room.

There are other action commands in the game. These include:

COMMANDS or 'E': this will print the moves that are legal in a given room. Not applicable in BOSS room
HELP: this will display instructions. Not applicable in BOSS room
INVENTORY: this will print your current inventory. Not applicable in BOSS room
LOOK: this will print the description for the current room. Not applicable in BOSS room
TAKE <object> or left click on an object in room object: this will take an object from a room and place it in your inventory. Replace <object> with the name of the object to take.  The object must be present in the room in order to take it. Not applicable in BOSS room
DROP <object> or left click on an inventory object: this will drop an object in your inventory. Replace <object> with the name of the object to drop. The object must be in your inventory to drop it. Not applicable in BOSS room.
left click on an inventory object in BOSS room: this will use the item to give you a buff.
'R': To open or close the objects in room display. Not applicable in BOSS room.
'I': To open or close the inventory objects display
'H': To open or close the player stats (health and strength bar)

The game is over when your player reaches the BOSS room and wins or loses.
